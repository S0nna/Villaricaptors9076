About Us

Villaciraptors 9076

Learn more about our team, our mission, and our journey in robotics.

Meet the Team

Villaciraptors 9076, a First Robotics team based in Montreal, QC, embarked on its rookie journey just last year. We started with just a handful of motivated students and mentors. Since then, we learned a lot from our experiences and challenges. We are now a team with about 40 dedicated members from grades 7 to 11. Each year, our team participates in the FIRST robotics competition, a world-wide competition for high school students.

Visions & Values

Our vision is to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators and leaders in STEAM fields. We value teamwork, creativity, and perseverance. Through robotics, we aim to develop not just technical skills, but also critical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication abilities in our team members.

Our Accomplishments

Since our inception, we've achieved significant milestones. We've successfully participated in multiple FIRST Robotics Competitions, earning recognition for our innovative designs and team spirit. Our members have also excelled in individual competitions, showcasing their growing expertise in robotics and engineering.
