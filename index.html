Our Story

Villaciraptors 9076, a Montreal-based First Robotics team, embarked on its rookie journey just last year. Our humble beginnings featured a handful of enthusiastic students and mentors. Over time, we've transformed, growing to a dedicated team of 40 members spanning grades 7 to 11. Each year, we eagerly embrace the FIRST Robotics Competition, a global contest designed for high school students. Through our experiences and challenges, we continue to evolve and learn, striving for excellence.

FIRST Robotics

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a non-profit global robotics community that aims to inspire and recognize students in science, technology, engineering, and robotics. They provide opportunities for students to develop skills in communication, leadership, and teamwork while engaging the robotics community. The main program offered by FIRST is the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), where student-led teams design and build robots to compete in challenges. FIRST also supports educators by providing resources and opportunities to promote STEAM education in local communities, preparing students for the future.

BetaBots

BetaBots is a unique program offered by Robotique FIRST Qu√©bec. It provides an opportunity for young Quebecers interested in science and technology to learn the basics of robotics through an engaging engineering and programming challenge. BetaBots utilizes components from the FIRST Robotics Competition program and aims to prepare participants and their mentors for the regular FIRST season. The program culminates in the BetaBots Competitive Challenge, where teams not only have the chance to compete for prizes but also learn and have fun in the process.
